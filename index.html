When to Visit

High Season

When: June and August

What to Expect:

  • Accommodation rates are at an all-time high, and any form of discount is unlikely to be obtained.
  • Winter also offers a little more rain, as it falls during the Northeast Monsoon season, but you won't notice much of a difference in the weather regardless of when you visit.
  • The Great Singapore Sale, which takes place in June and July, attracts even more visitors, with hotels and other lodgings packed.

Plan your visit to Bird Paradise

1. By Bus

Bird Paradise is open throughout the year, even on public holidays and selected school holidays.

Opening Hours: 9:00am - 6:00pm

Last Entry: 5:00pm

Best time to visit

If you want to avoid traveling to Singapore during high season (December-January, June-July), consider planning your trip between mid-January and May. During this time, attractions are generally less crowded. For an even quieter experience, opt for weekdays rather than weekends, as weekends tend to be busier with families and children enjoying their days off.

Address: 20 Mandai Lake Rd, Singapore 729825

Find on Maps

By MRT: You can take the train to any of these MRT stations - Khatib (NS14), Choa Chu Kang (NS4), Ang Mo Kio (NS16) and Springleaf (TE4). From there, you can hop on a connecting bus that will take you straight to Mandai Wildlife Reserve.

By Bus: To visit Bird Paradise at Mandai Wildlife Reserve, alight at bus stop #48111 (After Mandai Road).

By Car Or Taxi: via PIE & COr Taxi: via PIE & CTE Expressway. Duration: 30 minutes from the city.

  • ATM
  • Prayer Room
  • Wi-Fi
  • Locker
  • Bird Paradise is best explored on foot. Therefore make sure that you wear comfortable shoes.
  • With many different types of birds, shows, and experiences to explore, keep aside at least half a day to make the most of your visit.
  • Do not forget to carry sunglasses, sunscreen, and insect repellants.
  • You are not allowed into prohibited areas.
  • Treat the birds with kindness and respect.
  • Flash photography is not allowed as it might disturb the birds in their natural environment.
  • Shooting videos or clicking still pictures for commercial purposes without prior permission is not allowed.
  • Smoking is not allowed. You can use a designated smoking area outside the park entrance.
